# Sample Leave Policy Document

## Leave Policy - Talent Ops

### 1. Leave Types

#### 1.1 Casual Leave
- **Entitlement:** 12 days per year
- **Carry Forward:** Up to 6 days to next year
- **Approval:** Manager approval required
- **Notice Period:** Minimum 1 day advance notice

#### 1.2 Sick Leave
- **Entitlement:** 12 days per year
- **Documentation:** Medical certificate required for 3+ consecutive days
- **Approval:** Auto-approved for single day, manager approval for 2+ days
- **Carry Forward:** No carry forward

#### 1.3 Vacation Leave
- **Entitlement:** 15 days per year
- **Approval:** Manager approval required
- **Notice Period:** Minimum 7 days advance notice for 3+ days
- **Carry Forward:** No carry forward, use it or lose it

### 2. Leave Application Process

**Step 1:** Submit leave request through HR portal
**Step 2:** Manager reviews within 24 hours
**Step 3:** Notification sent to employee
**Step 4:** Update team calendar

### 3. Leave Escalation

If leave request not approved within 48 hours:
1. Contact team lead
2. If unresolved, escalate to department head
3. Final escalation to HR director

### 4. Emergency Leave

For emergencies:
- Inform manager immediately (phone/email)
- Submit formal request within 24 hours
- Provide supporting documentation

### 5. Leave Balance

Check balance through:
- HR portal dashboard
- Monthly payslip
- Ask HR chatbot: "What is my leave balance?"
