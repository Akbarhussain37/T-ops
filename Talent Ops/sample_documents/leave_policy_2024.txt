TALENT OPS - EMPLOYEE LEAVE POLICY 2024

===================================
Document ID: leave-policy-2024
Last Updated: December 2024
===================================

## 1. LEAVE ENTITLEMENTS

### 1.1 Annual Leave
- All full-time employees: 20 days per year
- Part-time employees: Pro-rated based on hours worked
- Leave accrues monthly at 1.67 days per month
- Maximum carryforward: 5 days to next year

### 1.2 Sick Leave
- Entitlement: 12 days per year (1 day per month)
- No medical certificate required for 1-2 days
- Medical certificate REQUIRED for 3+ consecutive days
- Maximum accumulation: 30 days

### 1.3 Casual Leave
- Entitlement: 7 days per year
- Cannot be carried forward
- Minimum notice: 24 hours (except emergencies)
- Maximum consecutive days: 3 days

### 1.4 Public Holidays
- All gazetted public holidays are paid
- If working on public holiday: 2x pay + compensatory off

## 2. LEAVE APPLICATION PROCESS

### 2.1 Standard Process
1. Submit leave request via Talent Ops portal
2. Manager reviews within 48 hours
3. Approval/rejection notified via email
4. Update calendar after approval

### 2.2 Emergency Leave
- Call/message manager immediately
- Submit formal request within 24 hours
- Provide reason and documentation if required

### 2.3 Notice Periods
- Annual leave: 7 days advance notice
- Casual leave: 24 hours advance notice
- Sick leave: Same day notification (before 10 AM)

## 3. LEAVE ESCALATION PROCESS

If leave request not approved within 48 hours:

**Step 1:** Contact your Team Lead
**Step 2:** Escalate to Department Head (after 72 hours)
**Step 3:** Final escalation to HR Director (after 5 business days)

Emergency leaves are processed within 4 hours.

## 4. SPECIAL LEAVE TYPES

### 4.1 Maternity Leave
- Duration: 26 weeks (6 months)
- Paid leave: Full salary for entire duration
- Notice required: 30 days before due date
- Documents: Medical certificate, expected delivery date

### 4.2 Paternity Leave
- Duration: 15 days
- Paid leave: Full salary
- Must be taken within 60 days of child's birth
- Documents: Birth certificate

### 4.3 Bereavement Leave
- Duration: 5 days for immediate family
- Duration: 2 days for extended family
- No documentation required for immediate family
- Immediate family: Spouse, parent, child, sibling

### 4.4 Study Leave
- Maximum: 10 days per year
- For job-related courses only
- Requires manager + HR approval
- Course must be pre-approved

## 5. LEAVE REJECTION REASONS

Common reasons for leave rejection:
- Insufficient advance notice
- Critical project deadline
- Multiple team members on leave
- Leave balance exhausted
- Peak business period

If rejected, you will receive:
- Detailed reason for rejection
- Alternative dates suggestion
- Option to escalate (see Section 3)

## 6. WORK FROM HOME POLICY

Work from home is NOT considered leave but requires approval:
- Maximum: 2 days per week
- Must be requested 24 hours in advance
- Manager has final discretion
- Must be reachable during work hours (9 AM - 6 PM)

## 7. NEGATIVE LEAVE BALANCE

- Maximum negative balance: 3 days
- Requires Department Head approval
- Must be recovered within next quarter
- Salary deduction if not recovered

## 8. LEAVE DURING PROBATION

Probation period (first 3 months):
- No annual leave allowed
- Sick leave: Only with medical certificate
- Casual leave: Maximum 2 days
- All other leave types: Unpaid

## 9. LEAVE CANCELLATION

Approved leave can be cancelled:
- Minimum 48 hours before leave start date
- No penalty for cancellation
- Less than 48 hours: Requires manager approval
