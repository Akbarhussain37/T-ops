## WELCOME TO TALENT OPS!

This handbook contains everything you need to know about working at Talent Ops.

## 1. COMPANY VALUES

### 1.1 Core Values
- **Integrity**: We do the right thing, even when no one is watching
- **Innovation**: We embrace change and creative solutions
- **Collaboration**: We achieve more together than alone
- **Excellence**: We deliver quality in everything we do
- **Customer First**: Our customers are at the heart of all decisions

### 1.2 Mission Statement
"To empower organizations with world-class talent management solutions that drive growth and success."

## 2. WORK SCHEDULE

### 2.1 Regular Hours
- Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- Lunch break: 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM (1 hour)
- Total work hours: 40 hours per week

### 2.2 Flexible Hours
- Core hours (mandatory): 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
- Flexible start: 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM
- Flexible end: 4:00 PM - 7:00 PM
- Requires manager approval

### 2.3 Overtime
- Overtime rate: 1.5x regular hourly rate
- Prior approval required
- Maximum: 10 hours per week
- Compensatory time off available

## 3. DRESS CODE

### 3.1 Business Casual (Default)
- Collared shirts, blouses
- Dress pants, skirts (knee-length)
- Closed-toe shoes
- No jeans (except Fridays)

### 3.2 Casual Fridays
- Clean jeans allowed
- T-shirts with company logo
- Sneakers permitted
- No shorts or flip-flops

### 3.3 Client Meetings
- Business formal attire
- Suits for men
- Business suits/dresses for women
- Formal shoes required

## 4. PERFORMANCE REVIEWS

### 4.1 Review Cycle
- Frequency: Quarterly
- Annual comprehensive review in December
- 30-day probation review for new hires
- Mid-year review in June

### 4.2 Review Criteria
- Goal achievement (40%)
- Quality of work (30%)
- Collaboration & teamwork (20%)
- Innovation & initiative (10%)

### 4.3 Rating Scale
- Outstanding: 5 (Top 10%)
- Exceeds Expectations: 4 (20%)
- Meets Expectations: 3 (60%)
- Needs Improvement: 2 (8%)
- Unsatisfactory: 1 (2%)

## 5. COMPENSATION & BENEFITS

### 5.1 Salary
- Paid monthly on last working day
- Direct deposit to bank account
- Annual increment: Performance-based (3-15%)
- Bonus: Up to 20% of annual salary

### 5.2 Health Insurance
- Coverage: Employee + family
- Premium: 100% company-paid
- Cashless hospitalization
- Annual health checkup included

### 5.3 Retirement Benefits
- Company contributes 12% of basic salary
- Employee can contribute 0-12%
- Vesting period: 3 years

## 6. TRAINING & DEVELOPMENT

### 6.1 Annual Training Budget
- Manager level: $2,000 per year
- Individual contributor: $1,000 per year
- Courses must be job-related

### 6.2 Internal Training
- Monthly skill-building workshops
- Lunch & learn sessions
- Mentorship program
- Leadership development program

### 6.3 Certifications
- Company sponsors job-relevant certifications
- Study leave available (see leave policy)
- Certification bonus: $500 upon completion

## 7. CODE OF CONDUCT

### 7.1 Professional Behavior
- Respect all colleagues
- No discrimination or harassment
- Professional communication
- Punctuality is expected

### 7.2 Confidentiality
- All company data is confidential
- No sharing of passwords
- Lock computers when away
- Report security incidents immediately

### 7.3 Social Media
- Don't speak on behalf of company
- Don't share confidential information
- Be professional in posts mentioning work
- Follow company social media guidelines

## 8. EQUIPMENT & TECHNOLOGY

### 8.1 Provided Equipment
- Laptop (refreshed every 3 years)
- Monitor (dual screens for developers)
- Mouse, keyboard, headphones
- Mobile phone (for eligible roles)

### 8.2 Personal Use
- Limited personal use acceptable
- No illegal downloads
- No streaming during work hours
- IT monitors usage

### 8.3 Return Policy
- All equipment must be returned on last day
- Damaged equipment: Replacement cost deducted
- Lost equipment: File police report + pay cost

## 9. REMOTE WORK POLICY

### 9.1 Eligibility
- Completed 6 months with company
- Consistently good performance
- Role suitable for remote work
- Manager approval required

### 9.2 Remote Work Days
- Maximum: 2 days per week
- Core team days: Tuesdays & Thursdays (in-office)
- Monthly all-hands: First Monday (mandatory in-office)

### 9.3 Remote Work Requirements
- Stable internet connection (minimum 50 Mbps)
- Dedicated workspace
- Available on chat/video during work hours
- Attend all meetings

## 10. TERMINATION

### 10.1 Resignation
- Notice period: 30 days (standard)
- Notice period: 60 days (senior roles)
- Email resignation to manager + HR
- Exit interview mandatory

### 10.2 Termination by Company
- Notice: 30 days or pay in lieu
- Severance: 1 month salary per year worked
- Immediate termination for gross misconduct
- Final settlement within 45 days

### 10.3 Exit Process
- Return all equipment
- Knowledge transfer (2 weeks)
- Clear all dues
- Receive relieving letter + experience certificate
